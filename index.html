About Me

Hey, I’m Vedant Johari! I’m not just a full-stack web developer — I’m someone who genuinely enjoys turning ideas into interactive, user-friendly web experiences. I started my journey in Mechanical Engineering, which might seem a world away from web development, but it's where I learned how to break down complex problems and find efficient solutions. That foundation continues to influence how I approach coding today.

I work mainly with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Python, building everything from intuitive interfaces to dynamic applications. One of my proudest accomplishments was collaborating on a team project where we combined creativity and technology to solve real-world problems. Working together, we built something that wasn’t just functional, but impactful — and I loved every moment of it.
